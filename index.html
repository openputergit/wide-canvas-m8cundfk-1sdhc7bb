About the Program

The BBA programme in the department of marketing provides students with comprehensive problem-solving skills in marketing. Our program covers regions across Jammu and Kashmir(J&K), Chandigarh, Punjab, Haryana, and the Seven Sisters states.

The curriculum includes:

  • Lectures and Case Studies
  • Workshops and Seminars
  • Research Projects
  • Management Quiz
  • Industry-focused Practical Work
